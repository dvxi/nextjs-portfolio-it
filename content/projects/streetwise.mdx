## User Stories

- **User with a disability**: Real-time routes that account for mobility needs for safe, efficient travel.
- **Mother with baby carriage**: Carriage-friendly routes for smooth travel.
- **Visually impaired user**: Audio-guided navigation and alerts.
- **Any user**: Notifications about route changes or obstacles (e.g. construction, uneven pavements).
- **Community member**: Alerts about nearby people who may need assistance (e.g. fall, wheelchair).

## Business Model Canvas (summary)

- **Value**: Safe, customised routing; real-time obstacle alerts; community assistance.
- **Segments**: People with disabilities; parents with carriages; general urban users.
- **Channels**: iOS/Android app; website; social.
- **Revenue**: Freemium; city/NGO partnerships; in-app advertising.
- **Key activities**: App development and maintenance; route data and optimisation; community support.
- **Partners**: City governments (data); disability and parental organisations; mapping/data tech.
