React-based website for the KERNEL20 student research group at AGH: anniversary milestones and a job board for students. Built with React; design and content developed with the team.

## Features

- Anniversary showcase: milestones and history of the research group.
- Job board: listings and applications for students.
- Responsive layout and clear navigation.

## Stack

- React for the frontend; static deployment for fast load and easy updates.
