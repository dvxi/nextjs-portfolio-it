## Introduction
"PukPuk" is a dynamic web application designed as a job marketplace specifically tailored for young individuals seeking opportunities in household tasks such as cleaning, laundry, or lawn mowing. This platform connects these young workers with households in need of these services, providing a simple yet effective solution for both parties.

## Sitemap

1. **Home Page**
    - Overview of PukPuk services and benefits.
    - Quick access to sign up or log in.

2. **Sign Up/Login Page**
    - Separate pathways for service providers (young individuals) and service seekers (households).

3. **User Dashboard**
    - Personalized dashboard with job listings, ongoing tasks, and history.
    - Profile management for users to update their skills and availability.

4. **Job Listings Page**
    - A comprehensive list of available jobs categorized by type (cleaning, laundry, lawn mowing, etc.).
    - Filter options based on location, pay rate, and job type.

5. **Job Posting Page**
    - For households to post new jobs, detailing requirements, location, and proposed pay.

6. **Application Page**
    - Where young individuals can apply for jobs and submit their qualifications and availability.

7. **Messaging/Inbox**
    - A communication hub for users to discuss job details and arrangements.

8. **Feedback and Ratings**
    - Users can rate and review each other, building trust and credibility in the community.

9. **Help and Support**
    - FAQs, contact information, and guides on using the platform effectively.

## Wireframe

1. **Home Page**
    - Eye-catching headline about empowering youth with job opportunities.
    - Visual icons representing different job types (cleaning, laundry, etc.).
    - Testimonials from satisfied users.
    - Call-to-action buttons for signing up.

2. **User Dashboard**
    - Welcome message with user's name.
    - Tabs for different sections: 'My Jobs', 'Inbox', 'Profile', 'History'.
    - A summary section showing active applications and ongoing jobs.

3. **Job Listings Page**
    - List view of jobs with essential details (pay, location, brief description).
    - Sidebar with filter options.
    - 'Apply Now' buttons on each job listing.

4. **Job Posting Page**
    - Form fields for job description, location, pay rate, and other requirements.
    - Preview and submit button.

5. **Application Page**
    - Form for applicants to fill in their details, experiences, and available times.
    - Submit application button.

6. **Messaging/Inbox**
    - Inbox layout with conversation threads.
    - Easy-to-use message composer with send button.

7. **Feedback and Ratings**
    - Star rating system.
    - Text field for written reviews.

"PukPuk" seeks to provide a reliable and user-friendly platform for young job seekers to gain employment in household tasks, while simultaneously offering a valuable service to households. This marketplace not only facilitates job fulfillment but also encourages skill development and financial independence among the youth.
