Sitemap and wireframes for PukPuk: a job marketplace connecting young workers with households for household tasks (cleaning, laundry, lawn mowing). Deliverables in Figma.

## Sitemap

- **Home**: Services overview, sign up / log in.
- **Auth**: Separate flows for providers (young workers) and seekers (households).
- **Dashboard**: Job listings, ongoing tasks, history; profile and availability.
- **Listings**: Jobs by type; filters (location, pay, type).
- **Post job**: Form for requirements, location, pay.
- **Apply**: Application form (qualifications, availability).
- **Messaging / Inbox**: Threads and composer.
- **Feedback & ratings**: Star ratings and reviews.
- **Help**: FAQs, contact, guides.

## Wireframes

Home (headline, job-type icons, testimonials, CTAs); Dashboard (welcome, My Jobs / Inbox / Profile / History, active applications); Job listings (list + filters + Apply); Job posting (form + preview); Application (form + submit); Inbox (threads + composer); Feedback (stars + review text).
