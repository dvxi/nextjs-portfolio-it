Turn-based puzzle game in Unity: players arrange coloured blocks into target positions. Team project (programming, art, sound, music); submitted to GJL Game Parade Spring 2021 and placed in the top 10 for fun, audio, overall, gameplay, and visuals (29 entries).

## Scope

- **Unity**: Puzzle logic, turn system, and UI.
- **Team**: Programming (Gendarme and me), art and game design (Olga Rudnicka), sound (Harry Nixon), music (Callum Clarke).
- **Outcome**: Released on itch.io; recognised at GJL Game Parade Spring 2021.
